## HeyGen Information

### Q: What is HeyGen?
A: HeyGen is the world's leading AI avatar platform. It creates photorealistic digital humans that can speak, present, and interact. HeyGen is trusted by Fortune 500 companies for sales, marketing, training, and customer engagement.

### Q: What is a HeyGen Ambassador?
A: A HeyGen Ambassador is an officially appointed partner authorized to demonstrate, train, and promote HeyGen's technology. CyberHopeAI and Rick Dallmann are Official HeyGen Ambassadors, which means we have special access and authorization to train others on HeyGen technology.

### Q: What can HeyGen avatars do?
A: HeyGen avatars can speak in multiple languages, present videos, answer questions, and represent your brand 24/7. When combined with CyberHopeAI's technology, they can also see who they're talking to, remember past conversations, book appointments, and take actions in your business systems.

### Q: What's the difference between HeyGen and CyberHopeAI?
A: HeyGen creates the avatars - the face, voice, and visual appearance. CyberHopeAI adds the intelligence - the brain that makes avatars know who they're talking to, remember conversations, and take real actions. We say HeyGen makes avatars that look real, and we make avatars that think real.

## CSA Program (Cybersecurity Apprenticeship)

### Q: What is the CSA program?
A: CSA stands for Cybersecurity Apprenticeship. It's a program offered through Hope Training Academy that provides free cybersecurity training to high school students in Indiana.

### Q: Is the CSA program really free?
A: Yes, the CSA program is completely free for qualifying high school students. It's funded through Indiana workforce development initiatives.

### Q: Who qualifies for the CSA program?
A: High school students in Indiana can qualify for the free cybersecurity training through the CSA program. Visit hopetrainingacademy.org/csa for details and eligibility.

## PrecognitionOS and SkillDNA

### Q: What is PrecognitionOS?
A: PrecognitionOS, or PCOS, is CyberHopeAI's AI platform. It's the brain that makes AI avatars intelligent by connecting them to business data, customer information, and workflows. It enables avatars to know who they're talking to and take real actions.

### Q: What is SkillDNA?
A: SkillDNA is part of the PrecognitionOS platform. It's our system for encoding and understanding capabilities - both human skills and AI agent abilities. You can learn more at skilldna.cyberhopeai.com.

### Q: What makes CyberHopeAI's avatars different?
A: Most AI avatars are like talking videos - they don't know who you are or what you need. CyberHopeAI's avatars, powered by PrecognitionOS, can recognize you, remember your past conversations, access your account information, book appointments, update CRMs, and take real actions on your behalf.
